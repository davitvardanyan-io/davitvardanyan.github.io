## Why I'm starting this blog

I've been working in software engineering for over 7 years. Over that time, I've learned a lot of good approaches and practices that really work. I want to share these with the community—both to help others and to document what I've learned.

Also, technology changes so fast. Every day there's a new tool, a new update, a new AI model. It's hard to remember everything that matters. By writing about it here, I'm creating a personal notebook that I can come back to whenever I need it.

## What to expect

I plan to write about:

- **Test automation** — Playwright, TypeScript, patterns and anti-patterns
- **AI & tooling** — Using LLMs to improve engineering workflows
- **Things I've learned** — Short notes from day-to-day work

Stay tuned. More posts coming soon.
